Why 4×4 Treadmill?

Everything you need for effective high-intensity interval training

💨

4×4 Interval Protocol

Based on the scientifically proven Norwegian 4×4 method: 4 intervals of 4 minutes at high intensity with active recovery periods. Customizable warmup, interval, and recovery durations.

❤️

Real-Time Heart Rate

Connect your Bluetooth heart rate sensor or Apple Watch. See your heart rate in real time with color-coded zone indicators so you always know if you're in the right intensity.

🎯

Live Coaching

Get real-time feedback during your workout. The app tells you to pick up pace when your heart rate is too low, or to slow down when you're pushing too hard.

📊

Execution Score

After each workout, get a score from 0 to 100 based on your intensity, consistency, and recovery. Track your progress and see how your performance improves over time.

📈

Detailed Analytics

View heart rate charts, per-interval breakdowns, distance, duration, and VO₂max estimates. Every workout is stored so you can review your training history.

⚙️

Fully Customizable

Adjust warmup duration, interval length, recovery time, treadmill speed, and incline. Set your max heart rate manually or use the built-in calculator. Supports English and Norwegian.
